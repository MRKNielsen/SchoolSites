Biodiversity & Ecosystems

Teacher slide decks for a 7-week unit on classification, biodiversity, Australian ecosystems, and conservation.

11 lessons · Written assessment (60 marks) · Spaced retrieval quizzes built in · First Nations perspectives throughout
Acknowledgement of Country
We acknowledge the Wurundjeri-Woiwurrung people of the Kulin Nation as the Traditional Custodians of the Country on which we learn. We pay our respects to Elders past, present, and emerging. This unit draws on Wurundjeri ecological knowledge as living, ongoing knowledge, not as a historical artefact.
